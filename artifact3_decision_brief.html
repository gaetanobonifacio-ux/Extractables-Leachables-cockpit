NVT-217 is not submission-ready from an E&L perspective pending remediation of one critical finding.

The principal driver is a confirmed N-nitrosamine (NDBA) leachable exceeding its compound-specific acceptable-intake-derived limit by approximately 3.6-fold in the drug product, trending upward over shelf life. A defined mitigation pathway exists (supplier formulation change), but it is unverified under sponsor conditions and its timing must be aligned with the filing strategy. A second, classification-sensitive finding (MBT) and several documentation gaps are actionable but not, on their own, submission-blocking.
